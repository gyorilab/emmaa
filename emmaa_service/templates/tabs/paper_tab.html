Description

This tab contains information about publications from which the model statements were extracted. The first plot shows the number of processed papers and the number of papers that support the model statements over time. Many papers do not produce any statements and the extracted raw statements go through a filtering and assembly process resulting in the numbers being different. Scroll further to see the titles of papers producing the largest number of statements and a list of newly processed papers. Click on the paper title to view the statements extracted from that paper. Click on the link in the second column to view the original publication and click on the “h.” badge to annotate the paper and view the publication text with evidence for statements highlighted.
